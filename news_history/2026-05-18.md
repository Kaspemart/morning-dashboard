*   **ECB's Balancing Act:** The European Central Bank (ECB) is still walking a tightrope, trying to tame inflation without tipping the Eurozone into a deeper slump. While some officials are hinting at potential rate cuts sooner than the Fed, persistent inflation in services sectors is making them think twice. Keep an eye on those inflation prints and forward guidance – they're gold for European bond markets.
*   **Political Tensions in Germany:** Germany is grappling with internal political friction, particularly around budget debates and energy policy. While not directly market-moving day-to-day, ongoing instability in the Eurozone's largest economy could create headwinds for the broader European bond market if left unchecked.
